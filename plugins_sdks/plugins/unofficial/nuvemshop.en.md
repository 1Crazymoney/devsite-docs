# Tiendanube

## What is Tiendanube?
------------

----[mlb]----
Nuvemshop is an ecommerce platform that allows you to receive payments with Mercado Pago.
You can offer your clients the [option to pay](#bookmark_set_payment_methods) with credit card or boleto and sell directly on your site or charge through the Mercado Pago site. 
------------
----[mla,mlm,mlc,mlu,mpe,mco]----
Tiendanube is an ecommerce platform that allows you to receive payments with Mercado Pago.  
------------
----[mla,mlm,mlc,mlu,mpe]----
Offer your clients to pay with credit or debit card, or their [preferred method of payment](#bookmark_set_payment_methods) and sell directly on your site or charge through the Mercado Pago site.
------------
----[mlu,mpe]----
> WARNING
>
> Important
>
> Please note that Tiendanube is optimized to operate only in the following countries: Argentina, Brazil, México, Colombia and Chile.
------------
----[mco]----
Offer your clients [to pay with](#bookmark_set_payment_methods) credit card, via home banking or cash and sell directly on your site or charge through the Mercado Pago site.
------------

### Transparent Checkout

It allows the **client to complete the purchase on your site**, without being redirected. 

----[mlb]---- 
1. Access the [payment methods settings](https://lojavirtualnuvem.com.br/admin/payments/) on your site menu, look for “Mercado Pago” and select “Edit”.
------------  
----[mla,mlm,mlc,mlu,mpe,mco]---- 
1. Access the [payment methods settings](https://mitiendanube.com/admin/payments/) on your site menu, look for “Mercado Pago” and select “Edit”.
------------
2. In the item “Type of integration” change to the option “Purchase process without leaving the site”. 
3. Finally, click on “Save changes”.

## Installments settings

----[mlb]----
Nuvemshop uses your Mercado Pago account financing settings. If you have made any changes, take into consideration that they will show on your site in the next 24 hours. 
------------

Tiendanube uses your Mercado Pago account financing settings. If you have made any changes, take into consideration that they will show on your site in the next 24 hours.

## Set interest-free monthly installments in your Mercado Pago account

----[mlb]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.com.br/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mla]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.com.ar/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mlm]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.com.mx/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mlc]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.cl/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mlu]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.com.uy/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mpe]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.com.pe/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------  
----[mco]---- 
1. Log in to your [Mercado Pago account](https://www.mercadopago.co/business#from-section=menu) and go to “Your business”.
------------ 
2. Access the option “Settings”, navigate to “Offer interest-free monthly installments” and click “Activate”.
3. Choose “How many do you want to offer?” and confirm the changes with the “Activate” button.
